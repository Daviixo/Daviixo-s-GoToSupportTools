Now, how do I use it?

Step by Step


1.- Type the phone number that will receive the SMS (Remember that you need to use the e.164 format (Example: 19177944300)).
2.- Input the text you'd like to send. (i.e SMS Test)
3.- Click the SMS icon. Wait a few seconds and the tool will send the message.
4.- Keep in mind that the information will be ready in your clipboard AFTER you click OK.

If it fails, please double-check the phone number, make sure it's a valid one. If the Phone Number doesn't exist, it'll prompt and error as well. You may reach me @Daviid under Slack or email at david.diaz@logmein.com if you've got any questions or would like to get more details..
