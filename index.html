About our Tools

Outbound SMS (Twilio SMS)

How to test the SMS Service?

This tool was developed thinking on how we could test the SMS Service after troubleshooting it. Perfect solution was to have a tool with a number that worked outside LogMeIn's network. The tool is pretty easy, please head to this page there you will find the download and setup instructions.

Outbound Calls (Twilio Calls)

How to test if a phone number is working?

As above, this tool was developed thinking on how we could check if a phone number was up and running. Please refer to this page to see how it works, the setup details and instructions on how to use it.

Product Notes

Simple but useful form to structure your notes!

Developed to make it easier to structure the notes for your calls. This was reviewed by GoTo's Staff Members before it's release. This will help you not only make notes quicker, but with a better structure. After you're done writing your note, with a simple click, everything will be structured and ready on your clipboard, all you need to do is CTRL + V or right click > paste!
